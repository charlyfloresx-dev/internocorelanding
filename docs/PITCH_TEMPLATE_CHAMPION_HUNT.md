## 🎯 IN-PERSON ANGLE (If Coffee/Networking Event)

### **SETUP (3 min conversation starter):**
```
"Hey [Name], I'm Carlos. I've built MES systems for Safran, 
Outset Medical... mostly fixing WIP and paro problems.

What's the biggest headache in your operation right now?"
```

**Why this works:**
- Not asking for business immediately
- Listening first (shows respect)
- Opens them to vent their pain
- YOU get real data for custom pitch

### **LISTEN LOOP (Then Ask One Follow-Up)**

```
They say: "We have too much WIP sitting in ensamble"
You say: "How long does it sit? And what's the bottleneck?"
They say: "2-3 days. Material missing from upstream orders"
You say: "So it's BOM explosion — stuff comes down to assembly 
         without all the parts it needs?"
They say: "Exactly!"
You say: "That's addressable. 60 days, free pilot. 
         I can show you how we fixed this at Safran.
         Coffee next week?"
```

**Why this works:**
- You validated the pain (they told you, not you told them)
- You named the root cause (BOM explosion = credible)
- You offered specifics (60 days, free, Safran reference)
- You asked for commitment (coffee = soft but real)

## 📊 TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION

### What to Measure (By Channel/Message)

```
LinkedIn Message (Timing):
├─ # of messages sent
├─ # of positive responses (yes/interested)
├─ # of neutral responses (maybe/no thanks)
├─ # of meetings booked
└─ Response rate = meetings booked / messages sent (target: >10%)

Email Sequence (Efficiency/Compliance):
├─ Open rate (target: >20%)
├─ Click rate on Calendly (target: >5%)
├─ Demo video click-through (target: >8%)
├─ Meeting booked (target: 1 per 20 emails)
└─ Conversion rate = meetings / emails (target: >5%)

In-Person (Coffee/Networking):
├─ Conversations started
├─ "I'm interested" statements
├─ Emails exchanged
├─ Follow-up meetings scheduled
└─ Conversion rate = meetings / conversations (target: >20%)

Overall Champion Acquisition:
├─ Total prospects touched: 50
├─ Total meetings: 5-10 (10-20% conversion)
├─ Total pilots signed: 1-2
└─ Cycle time: 2-4 weeks per champion
```

### A/B Test Your Pitch

```
VERSION A: Timing (Paros/Loss Aversion)
└─ "8 paros/month = $32K/month lost. We fix it."

VERSION B: Efficiency (Gain/Capacity)
└─ "30% hidden capacity. $330K-$440K free production."

VERSION C: Compliance (Risk/Regulatory)
└─ "FDA audit risk. One paro and you're exposed."

SPLIT TEST:
Week 1: Send A to 20, B to 20, C to 20 (60 total)
Week 2: Analyze response rate + meeting rate by version
Week 3: Scale to the winner. Keep A/B test secondary version.
Week 4+: Iterate on wording within winner.
```

**Target:** Identify best-converting message type by 2-week mark, scale aggressively to that.

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES (Avoid These)

| Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Generic "interested in meeting?" | Every SaaS says this | Lead with SPECIFIC metric: "$4.8M → $40K" |
| "We have a revolutionary platform" | Buzzwords = deleted | Talk pain: "paros cost $200/min" |
| Long wall of text | Skimmed in 3 sec, then deleted | Bullet point format, max 100 words/paragraph |
| No personalization | Looks like spam | Mention their company + specific problem |
| Demo link but no context | Why should they click? | "2-min demo of how BOM prediction works" |
| Asking for a meeting directly | Too high friction | Offer low-friction: "2-min video" or "Calendly if interested" |
| Weak CTA like "let's chat" | Ambiguous. No action. | "Book 15 min: [link]" or "Watch: [link]" |
| No follow-up | Most people miss email 1 | Sequence: Email 1→5 days→Email 2→7 days→Email 3 |
| Overselling features | Director doesn't care about features | Sell outcomes: "WIP reduction 99%" vs "multi-tenancy architecture" |
