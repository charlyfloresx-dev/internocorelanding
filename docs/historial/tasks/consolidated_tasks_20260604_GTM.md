## ⚡ KEY DECISIONS MADE

1. **Publish Landing v2.0 First** (don't wait for perfection)
   - Reason: A/B testing requires live traffic
   - A bad live landing beats a perfect offline landing
   
2. **Pain-Centric Copy Over Feature-Centric**
   - Reason: Directoress care about $K savings, not CQRS
   - Measurement: 87% fewer paros beats "multi-tenancy"

3. **Champion Hunt Over Cold Email First**
   - Reason: 1 case study = 50+ cold email equivalents (social proof)
   - ROI: 1 champion = baseline for marketing at scale

4. **Vercel Over Docker/AWS for Landing**
   - Reason: 2-min deploy, free, fast CDN, no ops cost
   - Cost: $0 until 100K requests/month
